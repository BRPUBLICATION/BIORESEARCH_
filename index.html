COURSE SYLLABUS

FIRST SEMESTER

  1. Foundations of AI and ML:
    • Introduction to AI and ML
    • Machine learning fundamentals
    • Deep learning basics
  2. Biological Data Analysis:
    • Introduction to biological data types (genomics, proteomics, etc.)
    • Data preprocessing and visualization
    • Statistical analysis of biological data
  3. Computational Biology:
    • Introduction to computational biology
    • Sequence analysis and alignment
    • Phylogenetics and genomics
  4. Pharmaceutical Data Analysis:
    • Introduction to pharmaceutical data types (clinical trials, etc.)
    • Data analysis and visualization
    • Regulatory requirements and compliance
  5. Programming for AI and ML:
    • Introduction to Python programming
    • Popular libraries for AI and ML (TensorFlow, PyTorch, etc.)

SECOND SEMESTER

  1. Advanced AI and ML Techniques:
    • Advanced deep learning techniques
    • Transfer learning and fine-tuning
    • Explainable AI and interpretability
  2. Applications of AI and ML in Biology and Pharma:
    • Predicting protein structures and functions
    • Identifying biomarkers and disease diagnosis
    • Personalized medicine and targeted therapy
  3. Project-Based Learning:
    • Students work on a real-world project applying AI and ML techniques to biological or pharmaceutical data
    • Project presentations and feedback
  4. Industry Insights and Trends:
    • Current trends and future directions in AI and ML for biology and pharma
    • Industry expert talks and networking opportunities
  5. Capstone Project:
    • Students work on a comprehensive project that integrates AI and ML techniques with biological or pharmaceutical applications
    • Final project presentation and evaluation
